About Us

Our mission is to empower individuals all around the world to take control of their own health. Our website provides our users with the tools to assess health status and receive personalised recommendations for follow up care if they display symptoms of a disease. It also addresses the needs of individuals living in rural areas by an established system that connects them with the doctors for remote consultation.
