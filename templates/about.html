Ethiopia

Ethiopia is a country located in the Horn of Africa. It is one of the oldest countries in the world, with a rich cultural heritage and history dating back thousands of years. With a population of over 110 million people, Ethiopia is the most populous landlocked country in the world and the second-most populous in Africa. The country is known for its diverse geography, including highlands, deserts, and tropical forests. The official languages of Ethiopia are Amharic and Oromo, and the country is predominantly Christian, with a significant Muslim minority. Ethiopia is also one of the fastest-growing economies in Africa and is considered a hub for innovation and entrepreneurship on the continent.
