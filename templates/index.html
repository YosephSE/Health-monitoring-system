About us

"We are a group of motivated and talented individuals from various high schools in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We recently participated in a 3-week Brighter Generation Python Programming Bootcamp, where we honed our skills and developed a groundbreaking health monitoring system as part of our community impact project. Our goal is to not only make a positive impact in the health sector, but to use our skills and creativity to solve other pressing problems within our community. With a drive for excellence and a passion for making a difference, we strive to create innovative and sustainable solutions for the betterment of our society. Our team is dedicated to using technology for social good and we are excited to continue making an impact."
