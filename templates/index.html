Services

We are pleased to offer a comprehensive range of medical services at Dil Frie Health Center. Our team of highly qualified and experienced medical professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional care to our patients. Below is a list of the services we offer to help our patients achieve and maintain optimal health and wellness.

Check Up

Blood pressure, Cholesterol level, Blood sugar level, BMI

Vaccination

Covid19, Polio, HPV, Tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis (Tdap) and Chickenpox vaccines are available.

General medical consultations

Medical advice, treatment for acute illnesses, and management of chronic diseases.

Maternal and child health services

prenatal care, postnatal care, infant and child vaccinations, developmental screenings, and family planning services.

Laboratory testing

Blood tests, urine tests, imaging tests, genetic tests, and more.

First aid and emergency care

For individuals experiencing a sudden illness or injury, such as cuts, burns, broken bones, heart attacks, or strokes.

About us

"We are a group of motivated and talented individuals from various high schools in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We recently participated in a 3-week Brighter Generation Python Programming Bootcamp, where we honed our skills and developed a groundbreaking health monitoring system as part of our community impact project. Our goal is to not only make a positive impact in the health sector, but to use our skills and creativity to solve other pressing problems within our community. With a drive for excellence and a passion for making a difference, we strive to create innovative and sustainable solutions for the betterment of our society. Our team is dedicated to using technology for social good and we are excited to continue making an impact."
